ABOUT THE OUTSIDE that you know and the outside that you don't I challenge myself to give you an idea. The idea is an online poetry magazine - a book of magazine verse - with contributions from outside: from the outside. A body of work will be amalgamated from such contributions as and when they are able to occur.

The jokes / Are ghosts / The joke / Is a ghost / How can you love that mortal creature / Everytime he speaks / He makes / Mistakes
JACK SPICER

SUBMISSION is work and is of work but it is also a calling and an art. Part of this art is finding a way to allow the outside to speak, which means submitting to a power that is on the outside. I believe a book of online magazine verse can be created this way. But in the end you have recourse to an editor.
